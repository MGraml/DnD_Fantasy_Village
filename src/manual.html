General ideas and navigation

Welcome to Assignan's (or maybe another medieval/fantasy village's) economy simulator!

At first, take some time and enjoy the hidden easter eggs on the page! Maybe you want to read a book? ;)

As you can see, Assignan has a complex economy and this website will facilitate the managing of all the resulting exhaustingly numerous difficulties.

The most important part is the main screen where you potentially see a request to load a .json file. In that case, switch to section 3 Your own village - Save and Load to start.

Furthermore, we have buttons in the right upper corner to adjust goods and buildings/sources of income, the already mentioned Save/Load buttons in the left bottom corner and below the two <,> - buttons for switching between the statistics in the main screen.

At last, you obviously already found the "Manual"-moon to this page and after you have loaded your village, you can also use the "Week passed"-sun to progress in time and watch your village growing.
